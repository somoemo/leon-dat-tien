CEO Leon Dat Tien - Nguoi Thuyen Truong Dua Sunwin Vuon Tam Quoc Te

Trong gioi giai tri truc tuyen hien nay, cai ten Sunwin khong con qua xa la voi cong dong game thu. Tuy nhien, it ai biet rang dang sau su phat trien bung no va uy tin vung chac cua thuong hieu nay chinh la tu duy chien luoc tai ba cua CEO Leon Dat Tien. Bai viet hom nay se giup ban hieu ro hon ve vi lanh dao tre day tam huyet nay va hanh trinh anh xay dung he sinh thai giai tri dinh cao tai website https://sunwinn.ltd/.

1. CEO Leon Dat Tien la ai?

Leon Dat Tien (ten that la Dat Tien) la nguoi sang lap va dieu hanh truc tiep cac chien luoc phat trien cua Sunwin. Voi kinh nghiem nhieu nam tu nghiep va lam viec trong linh vuc cong nghe tai chinh va giai tri so tai nuoc ngoai, anh da mang ve nhung tu duy quan tri hien dai, ket hop voi su thau hieu thi truong noi dia de tao nen mot san choi dang cap.

"Su menh cua toi khong chi la tao ra mot cong game, ma la xay dung mot moi truong giai tri minh bach, an toan va cong bang cho moi nguoi choi." – CEO Leon Dat Tien chia se.

2. Hanh trinh xay dung thuong hieu Sunwin

Bat dau tu nhung ngay dau khi thi truong game doi thuong con nhieu hon loan, Leon Dat Tien da xac dinh huong di rieng cho Sunwin la "Lay nguoi dung lam goc". Anh tap trung toi uu hoa ba yeu to cot loi:

  • Cong nghe bao mat: Ap dung he thong ma hoa SSL 128 bit va tuong lua da lop de bao ve thong tin khach hang tuyet doi.
  • Trai nghiem nguoi dung: Giao dien duoc thiet ke toi gian nhung sang trong, toc do truy cap duoc toi uu hoa ngay ca trong gio cao diem.
  • Su minh bach: Moi ket qua tro choi deu duoc giam sat boi cac to chuc quoc te, dam bao khong co su can thiep tu ben ngoai.

3. Tam nhin va dinh huong tuong lai cua Leon Dat Tien

Duoi su dan dat cua CEO Leon Dat Tien, Sunwin khong chi dung lai o mot cong game bai ma dang dan chuyen minh thanh mot tap doan giai tri da nang. Dinh huong trong 5 nam toi cua anh la dua Sunwin tro thanh thuong hieu top 1 tai khu vuc Dong Nam A, mo rong mang luoi lien ket voi cac nha phat hanh game hang dau the gioi.

Doi moi cong nghe khong ngung

Leon Dat Tien luon thuc day doi ngu ky thuat nghien cuu tich hop tri tue nhan tao (AI) de ca nhan hoa trai nghiem nguoi dung, giup goi y nhung tro choi phu hop voi so thich cua tung ca nhan.

Phat trien cong dong ben vung

Ngoai kinh doanh, vi CEO nay con chu trong den cac hoat dong thien nguyen va xay dung cong dong nguoi choi van minh. Anh tin rang su thanh cong cua mot doanh nghiep phai di doi voi gia tri dong gop cho xa hoi.

4. Tai sao nguoi choi tin tuong su dan dat cua Leon Dat Tien?

Su tin tuong cua cong dong danh cho Sunwin chinh la minh chung ro nhat cho thuc luc cua Leon Dat Tien. Anh luon xuat hien voi vai tro la nguoi giai quyet cac khieu nai, lang nghe phan hoi cua nguoi choi de khong ngung cai thien dich vu. Chinh su cau thi va quyet liet trong cong viec da giup anh xay dung duoc mot "de che" Sunwin vung manh nhu hien nay.
